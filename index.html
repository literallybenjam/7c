Sevensi

An etymologically distinct language by @literallybenjam

Sevensi is a fictional language created as part of The LANGDEV Project. It has been designed to be etymologically distinct from existing “real-world” languages for use in the construction of fictions and narratives. It has a unique culture, history, and linguistic tradition. It is open-source and in the public domain.

It is also currently a work-in-progress.

Efforts related to Sevensi development are currently focused on the completion of Classical Sevensi, which you can read more about here. This page will be updated with information as new developments are made.
